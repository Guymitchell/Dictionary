Field Notes

This view returns a selection of NOMTRANS records and columns designed to provide a list of all bills and credit notes broken down by bill components (costs, costs VAT, disbursements and disbursements VAT), including any outstanding values. The view includes a column for the number of days between today and the bill date to enable aged-debt information to be extracted from this view.

The POST-TYPE will only ever be "BC" for bill to client, or "CN" for credit notes.

The NET-VALUE and VAT-VALUE fields will always be negative for bills and positive for credit notes, whereas the OUTST-VALUE field will be positive for bills and negative for credit notes.

Note that if a bill has been forward-dated then DAYS-OUTST will be a negative figure.
