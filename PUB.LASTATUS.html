Practice Manager Interfaces

This table holds the legal aid status definitions maintained from Practice Manager.



Linking And Application

This table can be linked on the LA-STATUS field to the LARATES table to provide a band Description to a master legal aid rates table. It can also be linked to MATTR.LEGAL-AID for including the Legal Aid Status description on matter reports.
