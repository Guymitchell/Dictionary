Practice Manager and Connect Interfaces

A MATTRELATIONS record will be generated whenever you create a relationship with a matter from the Relationships tab of either a Client or Contact or Organisation or Matter record, and this entails the automatic creation of a reciprocal CLIERELATIONS, CONTRELATIONS, ORGARELATIONS or MATTRELATIONS record.

This does not include the process of creating client matters: no MATTRELATIONS or reciprocal CLIERELATIONS record is generated in these circumstances.

However there may be situations where there is a valid relationship between a contact (who happens also to be a client) and a matter other than the client's own, in which case a perfectly normal relationship can be made between the client's underlying CONTACTS record and the matter.

Field Notes

Maintenance of ROL-CODE field

ROL-CODE is invariably user-defined from the drop-down list of role codes offered when you create the relationship. Unlike the CLIERELATIONS, CONTRELATIONS or ORGARELATIONS tables there are no special values imposed by the software.
