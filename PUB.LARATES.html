Practice Manager Interfaces

This table holds the detailed legal aid rates against legal aid activity codes in your system. This maintenance data is accessed in Practice Manager from the "Activity Code Maintenance" screen for those activity codes that are flagged as "Legal Aid". In this case the "LA Rates" button becomes active, and clicking on this button will list the legal aid rates that apply to the selected activity code. Individual legal rates can be amended, deleted or amended by clicking on the appropriate button as shown below.








Linking And Application

This table can be used to provide a variety of master listings of Legal Aid rates grouped by ranking of Fee Earner (ie the FE-STATUS field, and thence by Fee Earner), or Activity Code or both according to requirements.
