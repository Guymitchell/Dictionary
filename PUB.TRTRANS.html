Practice Manager & Connect Interfaces

This table is updated by time posting and time billing processes, and contains individual time posting details of all unbilled time, billed time and fees billed.

Time is initially created in an "unbilled" state (TRTRANS.TYPE = 1) by the PM or Connect time posting screens:






Field Notes

TRANS-TYPE can be either 1 for unbilled, 2 for billed or 7 for the time bill entry itself (value of fees billed by fee earner). Note that there will a separate type 7 entry for each combination of fee earner and VAT code contributing to the bill, so that any one bill will be represented by as many TRTRANS records as you have fee earners and VAT codes in that bill.


ORIG-VALUE in relation to TRANS-TYPE 1 or 2 records is the original value when posted irrespective of any subsequent changes.

CURR-VALUE in relation to TRANS-TYPE 1 or 2 records is the value now after any amendments have been made directly via the View/Edit Time Transaction screen.

OUTST-VALUE is the value of time (compared to CURR-VALUE) which has not yet been billed, as it is possible in V4.17 to part-bill a single time posting

ORIG-VALUE in TRANS-TYPE 7 records is the CURR-VALUE of the TRANS-TYPE 1 record that is being billed.

CURR-VALUE in TRANS-TYPE 7 records is the chosen value of the fee to be billed.

BILLED-DATE and BILLED-YRPR let you establish the unbilled WIP on your books at a historic point in time without having to look up the period, year and date of your time billing records. (You can still look up billing transactions using the BILLTX field, but because of the sheer size of TRTRANS this is likely to be very slow and all but impractical as well as unnecessary).


Linking And Application


TRTRANS would usually be linked in a report to CLIDB, MATDB and MATTR to provide details of time recording and time billing activity grouped and/or subtotalled by Client and Matter. As both the MT-CODE and CL-CODE fields of TRTRANS are indexed, either can be used for linking according to the depth of summary needed.
