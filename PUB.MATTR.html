Field Notes

MATTR has moved beyond its original primary purpose of storing time-recording data and has become a wide-ranging extension to the main MATDB table, especially for storing legal aid related details at matter level (other matter-level details are held in MATDB and MATAC).



Note in particular MATTR.TOT-BILLED which is the only available matter-level measure of net costs billed per matter. Its sum would also be used in reporting the total of costs billed for the client. There are no other fields in the database which keep a convenient running total of matter costs billed at either matter or client level.

CRIM_CAT is not validated against the CATEGORY field in the table CRIM-CAT so any integer number would be accepted, with a default value of zero if nothing is done with it. If you join CRIM-CAT to MATTR in order to say list your criminal legal aid matters by this category you should therefore use an outer-join so that matters with a wrong or missing category can be exposed and dealt with.

ESTIMATES_LOG is visible from the matter Legal Aid tab. It contains one record per change made to a matter's costs estimate. There is no limit to the number of records possible. It comprises 4 pieces of information separated by spaces:

  • Date of change
  • Previous estimate
  • Date of original estimate (from MATDB.DATE-ESTIMATE)
  • User login of person making the change
