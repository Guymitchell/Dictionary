General Notes

This table holds one record exactly for each matter on your system, whether the matter is open, closed or archived. It is created automatically when a matter is opened, along with one MATDB and one MATTR record.


Field Notes

All 6 balance fields, both the total fields and the DATE-LAST-POST field are updated as a by-product of posting and cannot be updated directly by the user. D-LIMIT and the three default bank code fields are editable when amending matter details. MT-CODE is fixed, as is CL-CODE field unless you move the matter to a different client.

BAL-CLI, BAL-DEP, BAL-UC and BAL-UPC should always hold positive (unsigned) values.

BAL-UBD and BAL-UPB should always hold negative values.


Linking And Application

MATAC is usually linked to MATDB for any report where client matters and some or all their accounts balances are wanted, e.g.:

CLIDB.CL-CODE -> MATAC.CL-CODE

MATAC.MT-CODE -> MATDB.MT-CODE
