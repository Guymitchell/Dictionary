Practice Manager Interface

This table corresponds to the information entered in the PM Legal Aid Categories maintenance screen.




Linking And Application

This table can be linked on the CAT-CODE field to MATTR.CAT-CODE and then to other matter tables to provide routine matter and balance listings of Controlled Matters grouped or summarized under the category name.
