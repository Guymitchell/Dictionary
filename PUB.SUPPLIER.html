Interface Notes

This table corresponds for the most part with data shown in the Practice Manager Supplier Maintenance screen, including the linked Supplier Payment details screen.






Field Notes

SUPPLIER-CODE is the same data as PURCHASES.SUPPLIER-CODE even though the latter has a defined length of only 8 characters. They will link successfully despite any warnings you might get you create the link.


Linking And Application

The SUPPLIER table on its own does contain all the information you need to provide a mailing list to your suppliers.

Otherwise it is used simply to provide supplier names when used in conjunction with the PURCHASES and NOMTRANS tables:

PURCHASES.SUPPLIER-CODE -> SUPPLIER.SUPPLIER-CODE
PURCHASES.TRANS.NO -> NOMTRANS.TRANS.NO
