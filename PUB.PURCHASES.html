User Interface Notes

This table is updated as a by-product of posting to your supplier ledger with either type CI or AD/ADV/ADVU transactions, i.e. certain transactions on both nominal and client matter ledgers update this table simultaneously with their main full entry in NOMTRANS.


Field Notes

DUE-DATE is the NOMTRANS.POST-DATE plus however many days were specified in the Payment Period box of the Create/Amend Supplier screen (ie the PERIOD field of the SUPPLIER table) at the time the posting was made. Subsequent amendments to the supplier record cannot alter DUE-DATE retrospectively.

OUTST-VALUE is the gross amount outstanding now, including VAT where charged. For the original invoice value and other details you will have to look up NOMTRANS using the TRANS-NO field. The OUTST-VALUE field is subsequently updated if or when payment has been made to the supplier. It does not necessarily correspond to the OUTST-VALUE field of the NOMTRANS transaction indexed by the TRANS-NO field. For example an AD posting for £50 will have an OUTST-VALUE of 50.00 in both PURCHASES and NOMTRANS at posting time, but as soon as it is billed to a client the NOMTRANS.OUTST-VALUE will fall to zero, while PURCHASES.OUTST-VALUE will remain at 50.00 until the supplier has been paid.

INTERNALREF is the optional INTERNAL REFERENCE now available for Supplier and Anticipated Disbursement postings. The main REFERENCE field for both these types of posting is held elsewhere, in NOMTRANS.REFERENCE.
