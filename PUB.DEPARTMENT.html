User Interface Notes

This table holds department definitions as defined in Practice Manager department maintenance program:




Linking And Application

DEPARTMENT-CODE can be linked either to FEETR.DEPARTMENT or to MATTYPE.DEPARTMENT to provide a departmental grouping and/or summary for any report that returns or summarises information on a grouping of fee earner or matter type.

FEETR.DEPARTMENT -> DEPARTMENT.DEPARTMENT-CODE
