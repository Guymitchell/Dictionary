User Interface Notes

This table holds the role code definitions as set up in the Practice Manager Roles Maintenance screen:



Field Notes

ROL-DEFAULT and ROL-DEFAULT-TO may both take the following user-selected values:

Nif Default Role for/In Relation to is None
COif Default Role for/In Relation to is Contact
ORif Default Role for/In Relation to is Organisation
CLif Default Role for/In Relation to is Client
MAif Default Role for/In Relation to is Matter

There are also several predefined ROLES records preset by Practice Manager, not user maintainable, where ROL-DEFAULT only has these special values:

C*   (where ROL-CODE is also C*)

L*   (where ROL-CODE is also L*)

M*   (where ROL_CODE is also M*)


ROL-MAY-BE and ROL-MAY-RELATE-TO each consist of a single number equal to the sum of the following "scores":

1 (where "may be" or "may relate to" a contact)

2 (where "may be" or "may relate to" an organisation)

4 (where "may be" or "may relate to" a client)

8 (where "may be" or "may relate to" a matter)


ROL-CODE and ROL-REV-CODE are generally the user-selected Role and Reciprocal Role code, but there are a few predefined ROLES records preset by Practice Manager and Connect. All these records are for implicit relationship types, such as Client to Matter ledger or Marketing Details, or Contact to Client.

Note that C*, L* and M* cannot be deleted or amended by the user.

Linking And Application

List of individual Contacts showing and/or grouped by their role description:

ROLES.ROL-CODE -> CONTRELATIONS.ROL-CODE
CONTRELATIONS.REL-RECORD -> CONTACTS.REFERENCE-CHAR

- with record selection filter of CONTRELATIONS.REL-FILE = "CO"

List of Organisations showing and/or grouped by their role description:

ROLES.ROL-CODE -> ORGARELATIONS.ROL-CODE
ORGARELATIONS.REL-RECORD -> ORGANISATION.REFERENCE-CHAR

- with record selection filter of ORGARELATIONS.REL-FILE = "OR"
