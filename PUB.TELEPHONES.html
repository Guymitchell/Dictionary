Practice Manager Interface

This table holds telephone numbers and email addresses for individuals, organisations and clients.




Field Notes

The EXTENSION and NUMBER fields are not validated on entry so may in practice consist of any text or numbers in combination.

If you are adding or editing a telephone record in the Client environment, then checking the CLIENT INFORM box will disable the EXTENSION field. The EXTENSION field will then contain only the software-generated text "CLIENT INF".

TYPE corresponds to Client (CL), individual Contact (CO) or Organisation (OR).

SEQNO is unique and is a sequential number in steps of 1 generated by the software. It has no linking or reporting purpose.

TEL-TYPE corresponds to the radio button choice of Telephone (1), Fax (2), Mobile (3) or E-mail (4).

All of a client's numbers are kept initially only in this TELEPHONE table - they are not present in the CLIDB table. If however you choose to designate a number as the Client's Primary number then it will be copied into the CLIDB-TELEPHONE1 field. If you designate a number as the Client’s Secondary number then that will be copied into the CLIDB-TELEPHONE2 field. In both cases the numbers will remain in TELEPHONES as well.

All telephone numbers for individual contacts and organisations are kept in this TELEPHONE table at all times - unlike address information there are no phone number fields in either the CONTACTS or ORGANISATION tables.


Linking And Application

For a list of private client names, with the names and telephone numbers of their individual contacts:

CLIERELATION.CL-CODE -> CLIDB.CL-CODE

CLIERELATION.REL-RECORD -> CONTACTS.REFERENCE-CHAR -> TELEPHONES.REFERENCE (where TELEPHONES.TYPE = "CO")


For a simple list of organisations and their telephone number(s):

TELEPHONES.REFERENCE -> ORGANISATION.REFERENCE-CHAR (where TELEPHONES.TYPE ="OR")
