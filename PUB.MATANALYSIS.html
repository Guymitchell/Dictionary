General Notes

This table holds one record per matter per accounting year in which any accounting or time recording entries have been made. MATANALYSIS records are never deleted by the software and are not directly accessible or maintainable by users. Only CL-CODE may be changed by you, albeit indirectly, if you decide to move a matter from one client to another.


Field Notes

Each field or sub-field represents only the movement for that period, so to find a particular balance you must accumulate all prior period balances for that field for periods up to and including the one selected. For example it is now 2010 period 7 and you want to list unpaid bill balances at the end period 9 of 2009. Your selection formula must read all MATANALYSIS records with a YEAR of 2009 or less. You then accumulate for each matter all the BAL-UPB fields for records with YEAR up to 2009, plus BAL-UPB fields for the first 9 periods only for MATANALYSIS records with YEAR = 2009.


Linking And Application

MATANALYSIS would usually be linked to MATDB via the MT-CODE field to provide a historical list of client matter balances ie the accounts and/or time/WIP balances as at the end of some required period end.

The Period options in the standard Practice Manager Matter Accounts and Matter Time Balance reports have been written using the MATANALYSIS view extensively. To find out more about how balance accumulation is processed you should examine these at your leisure in your sosdocs\layout folder. The reports are named rpMACPER and rpMTRPER for the accounts and time versions respectively.
