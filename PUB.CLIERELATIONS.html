Practice Manager and Connect Interfaces

A CLIERELATIONS record will be created in any of the following circumstances:

  • indirectly, whenever a new client, whether private or business is created
  • indirectly, whenever an organisation or individual contact is added as a party to a client
  • whenever a relationship is created either side of which is a client

Before creating a new client the client must already be an individual contact or organisation. It follows that the creation of a private or business client produces a CONTRELATIONS or ORGARELATIONS record for the original contact/organisation record pointing to its own new CLIDB record. Similarly a CLIERELATIONS record is created at the same time for the new client record, pointing to its own original CONTACTS or ORGANISATION record.

Field Notes

Maintenance of ROL-CODE field

ROL-CODE has a user maintainable value only when you create relationships manually from the relationship tab of a Client record. You will already have a table of ROLES created in the ROLES maintenance program and this will be shown as a drop down list from which you must make a selection.

Special Role Codes - M*

A CLIERELATIONS record has a special ROL-CODE value of "M*" (inaccessible to the user) when the relationship is between the client and the organisations or individual contacts that form the parties of the client ("M" is for "Marketing details" and the "*" indicates that it is a system-defined role code). There will be a CLIERELATIONS record with a ROL-CODE of "M*" for every contact or organisation which is a party to the Client.
